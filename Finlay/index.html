Organisation

I have left this one to last as I think it is the most important

  • Use Google Calendar
    • Quick and Easy to set up
    • Works on every device
    • Means you always have your timetable on you.
    • Google Calendar makes it really easy to rearrange your events - which is great
  • Be really specific with your timetable
  • Don't be afraid to change it
  • Divide your time amongst your subjects

Google Calendar Tips

  • Put everything in it.
    • School classes timetable
    • Study times
    • SAC Dates
    • Exam Dates
  • Any other event in your life
  • Connect it with Facebook
  • Use the notifications features it has
  • Add it to your phones home screen (Android)
  • put everything in it.
    • School classes timetable
      • Initially I was just using it for my school classes timetable, but it was so convenient I ended up using it for everything
    • Study times
    • SAC Dates
    • Exam Dates
    • any other event in your life
  • Connect it with Facebook
    • Facebook offers two different calendar streams
      • One for all the events you are invited to on Facebook
      • and one for your friends birthdays, ( which is great cos it means you can easily see who's birthday is in the next few days and wish them a happy birthday if you bump into them - it makes you look like a truly great friend)
      • So Happy Birthday for Thursday Zach Stagg
  • Use the notifications features it has:
  • particularly for your study events That way even your phone is reminding you to study, so you have no excuse
  • add it to your phones home screen (Android and maybe iPhone)
