Exams and SACs

  • Do as many as possible
  • Get a copy of all the practice ones now - it can take ages to get a copy of them allm
    • if you cant get a copy of them all just yet try and get a list of them (or at least a rough number of how many you will have access to)
  • It's never to early to start doing them
    • start before you finish the subjects content in class
    • that way you already have a feel for what questions are important when you go over it in class
  • Log your results
    • (Surprisingly) incredibly motivational
